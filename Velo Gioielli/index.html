é circa il viaggio

It's all about the journey

Velo Gioielli is the innovator in cycle-inspired jewelry. Since 2009 company owner Brian has shared his unique passion of hand-made keepsakes that are memorable, durable and affordable. Velo Gioielli jewelery makes the perfect gift for bicycle lovers and outdoorsy fashion-forward individuals.

CUSTOM

Creating a custom sized spoke bracelet on the spot at events is only where custom starts. Many ideas are inspired by a customer's custom requests. Velo Gioielli has created pieces from wrecked wheels and used chain supplied by a customer, along with special sizing, color and specific bike parts. There is little more satisfying than seeing a custom request become reality with a happy smile on a customer's face.

DURABLE

Velo Gioielli only uses Stainless steel wheel spokes and chain. Bronze, copper or sterling silver are some of the primary materials used in the creation for many of the pieces. If steel parts are used they are powder coated in a durable coating to prevent corrosion. Who would want a rusty chain on their body? Durable quality is as important as is chic!

CYCLE-INSPIRED

From day one Velo Gioielli's creations were to be jewelry foremost but inspired by cycling. Velo Gioielli designs jewelry first then injects the cycling theme into it. Sometimes it is very apparent what the jewelry is from; other times there is just a hint that a piece of bike is used. No matter what, it is essential that the jewelry is just that, jewelry that anyone can wear whether working out or going out on the town.

PASSION-FILLED

Velo Gioielli is cycling passion defined in wearable art. Velo translates to "bike" in French and "gioielli" to jewelry in Italian. This is because of the passion of the sport in those regions. Then mix in years of riding, racing and touring and the passion to feel the pavement rolling beneath a bike being powered by nobody but you. Velo Gioielli creates the outward decoration of that passion through jewelry and art.
